07 — How

How to Use Bear Brand

Bear Brand is a ready-to-drink sterilized milk. To get the best taste and maintain its nutrition, follow the correct serving and storage guidelines.

1

Check the Packaging

Read the label, check the expiration date, and ensure the can seal is intact before consuming.

2

Drink Directly

Bear Brand is sterilized and ready-to-drink. You can consume it directly from the can anytime.

3

Serve Cold

Chill in the refrigerator for a more refreshing taste. Perfect for daily consumption and busy routines.

4

Serve Warm (Correctly)

Pour into a glass or mug before heating. Do not heat the can directly. Avoid boiling to preserve nutrients.

5

No Additives Needed

Bear Brand is designed to be consumed directly without additional ingredients or mixing for daily nutrition.

6

Storage Rules

Unopened: store at room temperature in a cool, dry place away from sunlight. Opened: transfer to a sealed container, refrigerate at 4–7°C, and consume within 3–5 days.

Daily Recommendation: One can per day is recommended for practical nutrition support. Do not consume dented, damaged, or expired cans to ensure safety.
